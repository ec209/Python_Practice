This is a rock scissors and paper game using opencv 
The trained sets has been already made so you do not need to preprocess the training images and make a trained sets out of it.

rock paper scissor and none xml files are trained sets

How to use:

python3 rockScissorPaper.py

put ur hand on the right angle of your webcam.
